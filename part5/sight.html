the window to the soul, they say:

What do your eyes tell me / Are they really a window into your essence / Such a mixture reflected there / Like a kaleidoscope / The focus shifting frequently / There / That looks like warmth / Concern now appears / Jealousy flickers on and off / A sense of humor / And a dollop of confusion / Anger blazes forth / Your eyes momentarily on fire / Vulnerability / Sensitivity to the feelings of others / Disappointment flashes / (In me or yourself / Probably both / But is quickly guarded) / Is that what I think it is constantly lurking / Yes / Love is always present / Sometimes only in the background / Other times almost overwhelming in its intensity / Yet always there / What do you see / When you look into my eyes / Self-doubt and insecurity / A relentless questioning of all dogmas / Combined with a searching for elusive truths / Tenderness / Affection / Caring / Vulnerability / Sadness / Jealousy / Anger smoldering / Always threatening to burst forth out of control / Trust in you / Hope for a future together / And a love transcending all time / Can our very substance increase over time / So that our windows / Outgrow their current limited views and depths / My belief is that / There are no proscribed parameters / To what we can achieve together / Our eyes can display a vista / In which stars can be seen to twinkle / Unicorns dance and distribute their magic / With every movement of their bodies / That we see the divine spark / In every person / And every living component of Nature / All is possible / Nothing is guaranteed / Gains can be lost / Souls can dim / Our eyes can assume a cold and vacant appearance / Continual nurturing is required / Assisting others in need / Honoring Nature in all its wondrous forms / We must choose wisely / So easy to stray from the path / Lured by greed for more things / Self-deceit in all its guises / What do people see / When they look into your eyes?
